About Us


BloodHub is a charge free association that seeks to put into contact people who are in immediate need of blood transfusion with those that are willing and able to give. Our Team’s Purpose is to make the communication between both parties easier and faster for the users. With our application being launched soon. People around Tunisia will be only a few clicks away from finding their donor.
